# Code of conduct

PatchLab documentation welcomes respectful technical disagreement and evidence-based
correction. Contributors must not harass, threaten, demean, discriminate against, or
publish private information about another participant.

Discuss the work, not the person. Assume an error can be corrected, state evidence and
conditions precisely, and allow people to revise a claim without humiliation. Audio,
DSP, accessibility, language, and learning backgrounds differ; questions asked in good
faith are welcome.

Repository maintainers may edit or remove abusive material and temporarily or
permanently restrict participation. Report conduct problems privately through the
repository's security-advisory channel rather than opening a public issue. Maintainers
will minimize disclosure, avoid conflicts of interest where possible, and communicate
the outcome to the reporter.
