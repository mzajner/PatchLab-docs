The curriculum moves from signal representation to systems, then from systems to
real-time engineering. Each mature lesson states an objective, prerequisite,
experiment, misconception, and primary reference.

## Lesson method

For every experiment, record source, level, sample rate, buffer size, channel layout,
warm-up, duration, and analyzer settings. Predict first. Change one independent
variable. Preserve a known-negative case that demonstrates the measurement can fail.

Listening is required when the lesson concerns perception, but level-match and state
what cannot be concluded from listening alone.
